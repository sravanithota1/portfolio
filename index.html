My Career

Film Expert

I also introduced many actors,actresses and technicians to the Telugu film industry, like Sridevi, Tabu, Tapsee Pannu, Manchu Lakshmi, Sreeleela, Venkatesh, Mahesh Babu, Allu Arjun, S. S. Rajamouli, and Marthand K. Venkatesh.

My Achievements

I got many Awards for my film makings and one of them is the state Nandi Award for Best Director for Bobbili Brahmanna (1984).I directed more than a hundred feature films across multiple genres such as romantic comedy, fantasy, melodrama, action thriller, biographical.

Mainstream

My mainstream works such as the 1987 social problem film Agni Putrudu, and the 1988 action thriller Aakhari Poratam, were screened at the 11th and 12th International Film Festival of India respectively in the mainstream section.
